How it works

Our AI-powered system uses a combination of speech recognition and natural language processing to transcribe and summarize patient-doctor conversations. The system is trained on a massive dataset of medical conversations, which allows it to accurately identify and understand medical terms and concepts.

The system can also generate summaries of patient-doctor conversations, which can be used by doctors to improve their diagnostic accuracy and streamline their record-keeping.
